Information

There are 2 main kinds of AI, Symbolic AI, and Connectionist AI. Symbolic AI are made with ifs , elses and other logic statements. Connectionist AI are made up of lots of information holders (called neurons) and have connections with each other. Each connection has a weight and that weight determines the likelihood of using the next neuron. Connectionist AIs are so amazing that they can do things that they haven't been programmed to do!
